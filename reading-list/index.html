Reading list

  • April 25, 2018

Books which were read, are being read, and some books… that have not yet come to be read.

Would Like to Read:

  • Clean Architecture by Robert C Martin
  • Programming Phoenix by Chris McCord, Bruce Tate, and José Valim
  • You Don’t Know JS by Kyle Simpson
  • Enterprise application architecture by Martin fowler
  • Untitled Sci-Fi Book by Christopher Paolini
  • Programming Pheonix by Chris McCord, Bruce Tate, and José Valim

Recommended:

  • HTML & CSS by Jon Duckett
    A beautifully presented introduction to HTML and CSS

  • Introducing Python by Bill Lubanovic
    An overview of python, also a good introduction to programming

  • Out of the Crisis and The New Economics by William Demming
    Principles of operations management from the guy who practically invented it

  • Clean Code and The Clean Coder by Robert C. Martin
    Advice for how to make programs that aren’t problematic, and how to not be problematic while making programs

  • The Hitchikers Guide to the Galaxy by Douglas Adams
    A (mostly harmless) book about a human trying to find the answer to “The Ultimate Question of Life, the Universe, and Everything”

  • Seven Pillars of Wisdom by T. E. Lawrence
    A book about a human who unites the tribes of Arabia to fight off the Ottoman overlords

  • Dune by Frank Herbert
    A book about a human who unites the tribes of Arrakis to fight off the Harkonnen overlords
